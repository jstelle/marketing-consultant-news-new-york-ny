What are the most common things that marketing consultants wish they had known when starting out in New York?

Marketing your business can be tricky, especially if you’re new to the game. One of the biggest challenges is finding the right person to partner with when things get tough. This is why consultants often turn to marketing consultants for advice. However, what most marketing consultants won’t tell you is that they wish they had known things beforehand that would have made them more successful from day one. Luckily, we’ve compiled this list of common mistakes new consultants make when marketing their business so you can avoid them!

You don’t need a website to be successful

Although it’s not a deal breaker, you don’t need to have a website to be successful. In fact, most small businesses don’t have a website because it’s too expensive and too complicated to maintain. If you can get by without a website, you can save thousands of dollars per year and avoid the headache of dealing with website hosting and maintenance fees. If you do decide to build a website, make sure it’s built on a platform that allows you to easily add and edit content, so you can stay on top of the updates.

Marketing doesn’t have to be expensive

Most marketing consultants will tell you that you need to spend a lot of money on marketing in order to get your business off the ground. However, this isn’t the case. In fact, you can get started for as little as $100 if you’re willing to put in the time and effort. You can also get creative with your marketing and make it fun instead of feeling like you’re stuck in a box. The best marketing strategies aren’t necessarily the most expensive ones either. For example, you can create a viral video for free on YouTube or Instagram. You can also use social media ads to reach your target audience for free. If you want to get more specific, you can also learn how to write compelling content for your blog, which can help your SEO.

You don’t need to hire a PR company

A lot of marketing consultants will tell you that you need to hire a PR company to get your business noticed. However, this isn’t always necessary. In fact, PR companies are notorious for charging high rates and not always doing their job. If you can get your business featured in the right publications for free, you can increase your brand recognition and boost your SEO. You can start by writing blog posts and submitting them to relevant websites. You can also try networking with other businesses in your industry to learn about their marketing strategies. If you’re not sure where to start, you can use a tool like PR Bar to find the top publications in your industry and sign up for a free account.

You can’t let your product or service speak for itself

This is a big one for new consultants. You need to make sure that your product or service is being promoted in the best way possible. If you’re not sure how to do this, you can hire a marketing consultant. However, you don’t have to spend thousands of dollars on a PR campaign. Instead, you can focus on creating content that speaks to your target audience. You can also try to find an influencer in your industry and make them a promise to give their stamp of approval to your product or service. You can also let your product speak for itself by making sure it’s of high quality and solves a problem for your customers.

You should always be networking

Another mistake new consultants make is that they only focus on marketing. While marketing is important, you should also be networking. In fact, networking is the key to success in any business. You can network with people in your industry, connect with potential partners, and make friends who can help you out in the future. If you don’t know where to start, you can join a networking group in your area. You can also join online communities and make connections with people from around the world.

Conclusion

Marketing your business can be tricky, especially if you’re new to the game. One of the biggest challenges is finding the right person to partner with when things get tough. This is why consultants often turn to marketing consultants for advice. However, what most marketing consultants wish they had known when starting out in New York is that they don’t need to spend thousands of dollars on a PR campaign. Instead, they can focus on creating content that speaks to their target audience and letting their product speak for itself. Now that you know what mistakes to avoid when marketing your business, you can start your journey towards success with confidence.
