What are the most common things that marketing consultants wish they had known when trying to scale their business in New York?

When growing any business, there are many challenges and obstacles that must be overcome. One of the biggest hurdles for companies is scaling operations to meet demand and hit new revenue targets. While this might sound easy in theory, it’s actually a complex process that involves hiring new employees, managing new processes and procedures, and finding new ways to reach new customers and markets.When scaling your business, there are many things that marketing consultants will advise you to do – from hiring the right team to setting up the right systems and protocols. While these are great pointers for any business owner, we also want to share some insights that might come in handy if you’re trying to scale your business in New York City.

Hire for culture, not just skills

One of the first things that many marketing consultants will advise you to do is to hire for culture. Culture is what makes a company unique, and hiring for culture is a great way to ensure that your company is able to attract the right employees and retain them for the long term.However, many entrepreneurs mistakenly believe that they need to hire for skills only. While skills are important, they often pale in comparison to culture. Culture is the DNA of a company. It’s what makes people come to work every day with a smile on their face. It’s what keeps employees engaged and excited about what they do. Culture is what makes a company great and able to scale.

Communicate, communicate, communicate

As a business grows, communication becomes even more important. The challenge for companies is that as the number of employees increases, so does the number of communication channels. As a result, it can be difficult for employees to keep up with the communication flow.When scaling your business, make sure that you establish clear communication protocols and procedures so that everyone has the information they need to do their jobs. This can be especially challenging if you have a team that is located in different cities. In this situation, you may want to consider implementing video conferencing software to help your team stay connected and engaged.

Have a dedicated team for marketing and sales

It’s important for companies to have dedicated teams for marketing and sales. Ideally, these two teams should be working together to ensure that the right customers are being reached at the right time.When scaling your business, it’s a good idea to have one team that is dedicated to marketing. This team should be responsible for all things related to marketing, including creating content, designing marketing campaigns, and setting up lead generation strategies. A marketing team is also responsible for measuring and tracking the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.A sales team, on the other hand, is responsible for selling products and services to new customers. The sales team is responsible for making calls, setting appointments, and closing deals. When scaling your business, it’s a good idea to have one team for marketing and one team for sales.

Understand the value of a good CRM

A good CRM (customer relationship management) system is an essential part of scaling your business. The CRM system is where all your customer data is stored and managed. This includes information about your customers, their contact details, purchase history, and product preferences.When scaling your business, it’s a good idea to invest in a CRM system that is robust enough to manage your business. A good CRM system will make it easier for you to manage your sales pipeline, track customer activity, and identify future sales opportunities. A good CRM system will also help you to forecast future sales, identify new sales channels, and identify new markets to sell to.

Find a mentor and work with them regularly

One of the best things that you can do to scale your business is to find a mentor. Mentors can provide you with advice and guidance on how to grow your business. They can also help you to navigate the challenges that come with scaling your business.When scaling your business, it’s a good idea to find a mentor who has successfully grown a business in the same industry as yours. You can find mentors on websites like LinkedIn and through local networking groups.When scaling your business, it’s a good idea to find a mentor and work with them regularly. This will help you to stay on track, overcome challenges, and achieve your goals more efficiently.

Conclusion

When scaling your business, it’s a good idea to hire for culture, communicate, have a dedicated team for marketing and sales, understand the value of a good CRM system, and find a mentor and work with them regularly.These are just some of the many things that marketing consultants will advise you to do when scaling your business.
